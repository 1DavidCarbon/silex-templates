### About Silex templates These templates can be edited with [Silex, live web creation](http://www.silex.me), the HTML editor and website builder for designers. ###How to customize it for your website? Download the [zip file](https://github.com/silexlabs/silex-templates/zipball/gh-pages) and unzip to a folder in your dropbox. Then [open Silex in a browser](http://www.silex.me/silex) and with it, open the template file ```editable.html``` from the ```/templates/``` folder. Then insert your content, modify the design and use it as your website. ###How to contribute These templates are made without programming, just drag and drop, with [Silex, live web creation](http://www.silex.me/). So feel free to [fork this github repository](https://github.com/silexlabs/silex-templates/), make changes and make a pull request. ###Creative Commons license These templates are licensed under a **Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported License**.
